The DeFi Financial stack


  1. SETTLEMENT layer.....Ethereum, Fantom, Solana (layer-1 blockchains)

  2. ASSET layer..........................ERC-20, ERC-721, ERC-1155, ERC-777, BEP-20, XRC-20, TRC-20 Asset layer tokens are a reference to it's native blockchain. eg Ethereum(ERC-20), Ethereum NFTs(ERC-721), Binance Smart chain(BEP-20) and Binance chain (BEP-2), Do you know what chains the other 2 belong, XRC-20 and TRC-20? If not, research them.

  3. PROTOCOL level....Aave, MakerDao, Compound (the essential mechanics of the ethereum Defi eco-system)

  4. APPLICATION layer..........Dapps such as Uniswap, 1-inch, Sushi swap

  5. AGGREGATION layer........Oracles(Price feeds) relays stats and financial information back to the blockchain/protocols

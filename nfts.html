NFTs. More than jpegs and Art!!


I believe NFTs are going to disrupt just about any and everything in years to come.


Imagine:

  1. Music NFTs where artists are empowered with royalty rights, and more sustained higher revenues whilst engaging more direct with their fans by airdropping concert tickets/NFTs, free goodies to loyal dedicated fans. Who knows, maybe you can even possibly one day be able to earn by just listening to music - 'GALA MUSIC'.

  2. Real-Estate on the blockchain. This will absolutely birth an abundance of liquidity into the property game with fractional investment in the form of tokenized assets.

    Imagine the deeds to your property packaged up in a nice NFT transported and stored with ease on the blockchain as you transact in BTC, ETH etc...How much time and money could that save when purchasing a property!! How about being able to own a percentage of a commercial building, being able to sell your share on the open exchange market as and when?!!...one example is - 'REAL T'

  3. DeFi Financial bearing NFTs. You could in the future potentially have a 'Bank Managers' NFT that enables higher savings interest rates with extra perks such as, a percentage of transaction fees on the protocol, enhanced collateral loan agreements/settlements, exclusive participation opportunities in other revenue areas of the protocol. The opportunities are endless at this point. - 'MELD'

    4. What other use-case ideas can you think of. Let's go outside the box!!
